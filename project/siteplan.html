Website Planning Document

Site Name

Name: Orvy

Description: Orvy is a beauty brand offering high-quality cosmetic products and skincare solutions. The website introduces the brand, showcases products, and provides beauty tips and tutorials.

Optional Domain: orvybeauty.com

Site Purpose

The website provides a platform to showcase Orvy’s cosmetic products, inform customers about ingredients and benefits, share beauty tips, and allow online purchases of select products.

Scenarios

  • Which Orvy products are best suited for my skin type?
  • How can I buy Orvy products online?

Color Scheme

  • Pink (#e91e63) – used for headings and accents
  • Gold (#f1c40f) – used for highlights and special offers

Typography

  • Poppins: used for all headings (h1, h2, h3) to give a modern/luxury feel
  • Roboto: used for body text and paragraphs for readability
